FAQ

What payment methods do you support?
Bitcoin, Ethereum and Dogecoin
We believe in the crypto-currency eco-system and strive to do our best to support it and the crypto community. For this reason, we only accept Bitcoin, Ethereum and Dogecoin for now as our payments. We may add support for other cryptocurrencies in the future.
Do you offer free trial versions?
We keep our licensing options flexible by offering several different choices. If you are interested in trying our services before purchasing a full license, try our discounted 14-day trial license or use one of our free public demo servers.
How much time it takes to get the cards once I completed my order?
We guarantee 5-10 minutes upon confirmation of payment on the blockchain and you will be notified and received the files from your email address.
